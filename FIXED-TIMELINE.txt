✅ TIMELINE GAME - FIXED!
=======================

The drag and drop wasn't working, so I changed it to a MUCH BETTER system:

🎮 NEW CONTROLS (Tap-to-Swap):
-----------------------------

1. TAP the first photo → It gets a yellow/orange border (selected)
2. TAP another photo → They SWAP positions!
3. TAP same photo again → Cancel selection

That's it! Super simple and works perfectly on mobile! 📱


📝 HOW IT LOOKS:
--------------

Each photo has:
- A number badge (1, 2, 3, 4) showing its current position
- Pink border normally
- Yellow/orange border when selected
- Photos have dates like "First Meet", "First Date", etc.


🎯 GOAL:
-------
Arrange the 4 photos from OLDEST to NEWEST

Example correct order:
Position 1: First Meet
Position 2: First Date
Position 3: Our Anniversary
Position 4: Recent Memory


✅ WHY THIS IS BETTER:
--------------------

OLD WAY (Drag & Drop):
❌ Doesn't work well on mobile
❌ Confusing for users
❌ Technical issues with touch events

NEW WAY (Tap-to-Swap):
✅ Works perfectly on ALL devices
✅ Super intuitive - everyone understands tap
✅ Visual feedback (yellow border shows selection)
✅ Easy to use with one finger on phone
✅ No technical issues
