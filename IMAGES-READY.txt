📷 TO REPLACE WITH YOUR PHOTOS:
------------------------------

Option 1: Replace the SVG files
--------------------------------
Delete the .svg files and add your own:
- photo1.jpg (or .png)
- photo2.jpg
- photo3.jpg
- photo4.jpg
- puzzle.jpg
- gift.jpg

Then update js/main.js to change .svg to .jpg:
- Line 353-355: Change photo1.svg to photo1.jpg
- Line 413-416: Change photo1-4.svg to photo1-4.jpg
- Line 538: Change puzzle.svg to puzzle.jpg

And update index.html:
- Line 15: Change gift.svg to gift.jpg
- Line 127: Change gift.svg to gift.jpg


Option 2: Overwrite the SVG files
----------------------------------
Just rename your photos to match:
- YourPhoto1.jpg → photo1.svg (yes, keep .svg extension!)
- YourPhoto2.jpg → photo2.svg
- etc.

The browser will display JPG even with .svg extension!
