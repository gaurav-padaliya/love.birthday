## 🎮 The 8 Games

1. **Tap Hearts** - Easy warm-up
2. **Word Scramble** - Unscramble BEAUTIFUL, FOREVER, etc.
3. **Love Quiz** - Obvious answer questions (Who loves you more? ME!)
4. **Memory Match** - Match photo pairs
5. **Timeline Sort** - Drag to arrange chronologically
6. **Complete the Line** - Fill in romantic phrases
7. **Sliding Puzzle** - 3x3 tile puzzle (hardest!)
8. **Final Reveal** - GIFT + YOUR MESSAGE! 💕

Each completed game unblurs the gift photo more!
