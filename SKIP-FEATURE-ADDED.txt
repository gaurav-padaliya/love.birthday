✅ SKIP FEATURE ADDED TO PUZZLE!
================================

🎮 NEW FEATURE: Skip Sliding Puzzle After 5 Shuffles
---------------------------------------------------

The sliding puzzle (Game 7) is the hardest game.
Now there's a way to skip it if it's too difficult!


🎯 HOW IT WORKS:
--------------

1. Game 7 starts with the puzzle shuffled (count = 1)

2. Player can click "Shuffle Again" to get a new puzzle
   - Each shuffle increases the counter

3. After shuffling 5 times total:
   - Counter shows: "Shuffled 5 times - Skip available!"
   - A GREEN "Skip Puzzle ⏭️" button appears
   - Button pulses to draw attention

4. Click "Skip Puzzle" to move to Game 8 (Final Reveal)


✨ VISUAL DESIGN:
---------------

Shuffle Counter:
- Shows at top of puzzle controls
- Pink text, bold
- Example: "Shuffled 3 times"
- After 5: "Shuffled 5 times - Skip available!"

Skip Button:
- Green gradient (stands out!)
- Pulses with animation
- Only appears after 5 shuffles
- Text: "Skip Puzzle ⏭️"


💡 WHY THIS IS GOOD:
------------------

✅ Players won't get frustrated if puzzle is too hard
✅ Ensures she can see your final message
✅ Doesn't appear immediately (must try 5 times)
✅ Rewards effort but provides escape hatch
✅ Better user experience


🎮 GAME FLOW:
-----------

Option 1 - She solves it:
→ Completes puzzle normally
→ Moves to Game 8

Option 2 - She needs help:
→ Shuffles 5 times trying different puzzles
→ Skip button appears
→ Can skip to Game 8
