OPTIONAL: Add Background Music
==============================

To add romantic background music to the game:

1. Find a romantic song (MP3 format)
   - Could be "your song" together
   - Or any soft romantic instrumental
   - Search "romantic background music" on YouTube

2. Name the file: romantic-music.mp3

3. Place it in this folder (assets/sounds/)

4. Optional: Add success.mp3 for game completion sounds

The game will work perfectly fine WITHOUT music!
Music just adds an extra romantic touch.

Where to get royalty-free music:
- YouTube Audio Library
- Pixabay Music
- Free Music Archive
- Or use any song you own

Note: The music will loop continuously in the background.
